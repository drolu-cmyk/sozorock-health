# Security policy

## Reporting a vulnerability

Please do not file public issues for vulnerabilities, potential privacy exposures, authentication weaknesses, or possible disclosure-control failures. Contact The SozoRock Foundation, Inc. through an approved private reporting channel and include a concise reproduction description, affected area, and any suggested mitigation.

## Scope

Reports are especially important when they could affect resident privacy, provider-state verification, CB-CAP aggregation or exports, shared-device kiosk resets, authentication, consent, AI controls, or deployment automation.

## Responsible handling

Do not access data beyond what is necessary to demonstrate an issue. Do not share, download, modify, or retain resident, provider, county, or credential data.
