## Full description

Care may exist. The path to it may not be clear.

SozoRock Health helps people prepare to use existing healthcare, public-health, digital, and community-support systems more effectively. The app offers a simple starting point for residents who need to understand what to do next.

Use SozoRock Health to:

- describe what you are preparing for by speaking or tapping;
- prepare for a virtual visit or other provider-led service;
- explore Library, Community-Based, and Home-Based Health Equity Hub pathways;
- identify digital-access and community-support needs;
- use the experience in English or Spanish;
- continue with clear on-screen controls when voice input is not available.

SozoRock Health is designed for varying levels of digital confidence, language, connectivity, and mobility. Large controls, plain language, screen-reader support, and privacy resets make the experience suitable for personal devices and supported tablet settings.

SozoRock Health is not a clinic, healthcare provider, or telehealth platform. It does not diagnose, treat, prescribe, triage symptoms, or provide emergency services. Licensed care remains with licensed professionals. If this is an emergency, call 911.
