# Option 2 storyboard — 80 seconds

## Visual system

- Warm paper, ink, moss, muted clay, and one restrained gold accent
- A continuous hand-drawn path is the recurring visual device
- Editorial typography with short, readable phrases
- Authentic community, rural, research, and public-system imagery only
- No doctors posing, patient-treatment imagery, artificial hospital scenes, floating UI cards, or synthetic portraits
- One focal idea per scene; key content remains inside platform-safe margins

## Scene plan

### 00:00–00:08 — Uncertainty

**Narration:** “Finding care should not require understanding every part of the health system.”

**Visual:** A quiet warm field. Several incomplete route fragments appear but do not connect. One person is represented abstractly by a small, human-scale marker—not a patient photograph.

**On-screen text:** Finding care should not require a map of the entire system.

### 00:08–00:16 — Where to begin

**Narration:** “But for many people, the hardest step is knowing where to begin.”

**Visual:** The fragments settle. One starting point becomes clear. The path begins moving deliberately rather than rapidly.

**On-screen text:** Where do I begin?

### 00:16–00:25 — A path may still be missing

**Narration:** “A service may exist. A licensed practitioner may be available. Yet the path between a person and that care may still be difficult to follow.”

**Visual:** Community resources and a licensed-practitioner destination appear at opposite ends of the frame. The line between them is incomplete.

**On-screen text:** Care may exist. The path may not be clear.

### 00:25–00:31 — SozoRock Health

**Narration:** “SozoRock Health helps make that path clearer.”

**Visual:** The path resolves into one confident route. Use the approved logo lockup only.

**On-screen text:** A clearer path.

### 00:31–00:42 — Start, navigate, connect

**Narration:** “We help people understand practical next steps, identify appropriate resources, and connect with licensed care where needed.”

**Visual:** The path moves through three large, uncluttered states: Start → Navigate → Connect. Show a community location, a practical resource choice, and a provider-owned destination without presenting a clinical decision.

**On-screen text:** Start → Navigate → Connect

### 00:42–00:50 — The boundary

**Narration:** “We do not diagnose, treat, or prescribe. Licensed care stays with licensed professionals.”

**Visual:** A clear boundary line separates non-clinical access support from provider-led care. The route crosses only through an explicit handoff.

**On-screen text:** Licensed care stays with licensed professionals.

### 00:50–01:01 — Systems insight

**Narration:** “Our work also helps communities, institutions, and public systems understand where access breaks down—and what can be done about it.”

**Visual:** Pull back from one route to a restrained pattern of de-identified paths across community settings. A planning table, research note, and simple regional diagram appear as authentic editorial evidence—not a fabricated dashboard.

**On-screen text:** See where the path breaks down.

### 01:01–01:10 — Work together

**Narration:** “Through research, partnerships, and practical implementation, SozoRock Health is working toward a simpler goal:”

**Visual:** Research, community, and institutional markers join one shared route. If an approved image or footage of Oluwabiyi Adeyemo is supplied, it may appear here without generative alteration.

**On-screen text:** Research. Partnership. Practical implementation.

### 01:10–01:17 — The goal

**Narration:** “A clearer path to care that already exists.”

**Visual:** The route reaches a calm open field and holds. Allow a natural pause.

**On-screen text:** A clearer path to care that already exists.

### 01:17–01:20 — Signature

**Narration:** “SozoRock Health. An initiative of The SozoRock Foundation.”

**Visual:** Approved SozoRock Health lockup, Foundation line, and website address. Music resolves gently.

**On-screen text:** SozoRock Health · An initiative of The SozoRock Foundation Inc.

## Social reframing

- **9:16:** Keep the route vertical and the focal marker centered. Captions occupy the lower-middle safe zone, above platform controls.
- **4:5:** Use the full editorial composition and preserve generous top and bottom margins.
- **16:9:** Let the path travel horizontally; do not fill unused width with secondary messages.
- Keep headline text at least 84px at 1080px width and supporting text at least 44px where it must be read.
