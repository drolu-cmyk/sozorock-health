A care journey can begin with something simple: being heard.

Meet Maya. Today, getting to care means distance, time, and one more unfamiliar system. At a trusted Health Equity Hub, she opens SozoRock Health. On a Health Access Day, trained local support and a prepared provider pathway are ready around her.

The voice guide says, “Take your time. You can speak, tap, or type.” Maya says, “I need help finding care near me.” The guide listens, then confirms her request in plain language.

With her permission, the Resident Access Layer checks her county, language preference, digital readiness, and the providers ready to serve New York residents. A clear option appears. Maya chooses it, then moves securely to the provider’s own platform.

SozoRock does not diagnose, prescribe, or replace care. Clinical decisions remain with the licensed provider. The platform simply makes the path easier to understand and follow—through a library hub, a community hub, or from home.

One calm conversation. One clearer next step. SozoRock Health: access where people live.
